Before You Reach Out

Frequently Asked Questions

A few things authors usually ask before their first call with us.

How soon will I hear back after I send a message?

Within 24 hours on business days. If you call during office hours, you'll usually reach someone directly.

Do I need a finished manuscript to reach out?

No. Many authors contact us with just an idea or a partial draft — our discovery call is designed to help you figure out next steps either way.

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes. The first call is a no-obligation conversation about your project, your goals, and whether we're a good fit for each other.

Can I request a specific editor or designer?

Absolutely — if you've worked with someone on our team before, let us know in your message and we'll do our best to match you again.
